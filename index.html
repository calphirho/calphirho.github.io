Engineering the Future

Welcome to the Phi Sigma Rho Chapter of Alpha Theta at UC Berkeley, a social sorority for women in engineering and engineering technologies.

Our History

Founded in 1984, Phi Sigma Rho was created by two women who found it difficult to participate in traditional sorority rush due to their demanding academic fields. Phi Sigma Rho is now established at 30 universities across the country.

Our Vision

We continue to encourage our sisters to develop the highest standard of personal integrity, strive for academic excellence, and build friendships that will last a lifetime.
